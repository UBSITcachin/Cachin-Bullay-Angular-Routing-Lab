Welcome to the Regular Show Universe
What is Regular Show?

Regular Show is an animated television series that aired on Cartoon Network from 2010 to 2017. Created by J.G. Quintel, the show follows the adventures of Mordecai, a laid-back blue jay, and Rigby, a hyperactive raccoon, who work as groundskeepers at a park.

Why do we love this show?

Despite its over-the-top and fictional nature, this show highlights the common life of young adults who are just trying to have fun despite the stressful nature of life. Many of its episodes also have life lessons hidden behind its surreal and unrealistic nature.

Characters of the show (partial)

Mordecai

A responsible yet easily distracted blue jay, often finds himself caught between work and his personal life, especially when it comes to romance.

Rigby

a hyperactive and impulsive raccoon, is his best friend and partner in slacking off. Together, they constantly try to avoid work at the park, only to end up in absurd and often supernatural situations.

Pops

A kind, naive lollipop-headed man with a childlike personality who owns the park.

Muscle Man

A loud and obnoxious park worker known for his 'My Mom' jokes and unpredictable behavior.

Skips

An immortal yeti with immense strength and wisdom, always helping the crew get out of trouble.

Benson

The grumpy yet responsible gumball machine who manages the park and often scolds Mordecai and Rigby.
